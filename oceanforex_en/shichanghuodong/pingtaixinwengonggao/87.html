Dove or hawk, a thousand people have one thousand Fed

Minutes of the Fed meeting in New York time, there is no doubt become the focus of the market day, but, at least from the perspective of market volatility, the market's view of the minutes of the very inconsistent.
 
Today's Fed minutes said nothing seemed, but a little. "The silence sound" means. Consistent with the recent controversy direction of the market, the weak economic problems in the first quarter of the Fed officials' focus of debate. However, minutes of the meeting released most are commonplace, the first is that we have become accustomed to the conclusion: "Most officials expect the economy and employment will improve in the first quarter after a weak performance, most officials believe that inflation will rise to 2%", this Conclusions in no way surprising, after all, a number of Fed officials have recently come forward to stand, this view is that they have in common speech.
 
Minutes of view of the US economy may be said to be mixed, on the one hand, the Fed acknowledged that "the economic outlook uncertainty has increased" and "export decline may reflect the impact of a strong dollar," "consumer spending led some Officials worry "; but on the other side, they still represent the majority of the current problem is influenced by temporary factors, Fed officials even optimistic medium-term GDP growth forecast raised.
 
Of course, most of the content is worth pondering: Most Fed officials believe can not raise interest rates in June, the Fed has not ruled out the possibility of rate hike in June.
 
Faced with the Fed meeting minutes, market volatility evident lack of direction, and instantly, non-US currencies meeting minutes released hikes, the euro / dollar exchange rate instantly rebound more than 40 points, a strong intraday dollar / yen again fell below 121, the price of gold rebounded to $ 1,213 / ounce, US stocks also rose rapidly, the Dow rose from the intraday low of 18272 points 18350 points; however did not last long, and then the dollar has recovered part of previous gains by the close, the dollar / yen exchange rate again rose to 121.30, the euro / dollar again fell below 1.1100, the Dow turned down 0.15 percent to close at 18,285.47 points.
 
From the absolute sense, the current economic data, the Federal Reserve interest rate decision in June rate hike unlikely in the face of weak global inflation strange, loose wave is now mainstream, and with respect to last year half of the fiery state, the US economy is also a bit "absent-minded", meeting minutes clearly Fed officials expressed the mainstream view: "The majority of Fed officials believe can not raise interest rates in June, a few officials at the meeting that the data may support the rate hike in June . " This view did not make the market surprises, according to interest rate futures data show that the market for the June rate hike expectations have been reduced to almost zero. However, the minutes of the meeting, the most noteworthy is not a simple hike or not, but the attitude of the Fed's long-term policy in the last interest rate decision, the Fed always stressed that his liberal stance many times, whether it is "long-term low interest rates guarantee "or" patient wording "are trying to clarify the Fed's own" doves "status, but the minutes of the meeting at the Fed's modest position, hawks seem to imply a heart, the face of the current economic data, the Fed is still reaffirmed "does not exclude the possibility of hike in June", the focus changes, perhaps the entire hike will affect the future path of the Fed.
 
Since last year, when the Federal Reserve to raise interest rates for the debate is always in focus, it can be expected in the next few years, this topic will also continue. Just released minutes of the meeting clearly not the climax of this drama, at best, only be regarded as an appetizer, next month, it will begin the real tit for tat may be staged. Former US Treasury Secretary John Connally famously "the dollar is our currency, but your problem" could not be more fitting.
